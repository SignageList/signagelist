SignageList is a curated open dataset of digital signage software products. It provides structured data on 548+ products including pricing, supported platforms, headquarters, and more.

## Key Pages

- [Product Directory]({{ .Site.BaseURL }}products/): Filterable table of all products
- [Categories]({{ .Site.BaseURL }}categories/): Products grouped by type (CMS, Content provider, Computer vision)
- [Platforms]({{ .Site.BaseURL }}platforms/): Products by supported platform
- [Full LLM Context]({{ .Site.BaseURL }}llms-full.txt): Complete product data in text format

## Product Data Schema

Each product includes: name, description, website, year_founded, headquarters, open_source (boolean), self_signup (boolean), discontinued (boolean), categories, platforms, pricing models (delivery type, plans with monthly/yearly pricing), screen statistics, and more.

## Categories

- CMS: Content Management Systems for digital signage
- Content provider: Ready-made content for digital signage
- Computer vision: Vision and analytics for digital signage
